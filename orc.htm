Titan
is the epitome of raw physical power on the battlefield. With unparalleled strength and devastating melee attacks, Titans excel in dealing massive burst damage, especially in close combat. Their ability to enhance their physical prowess through powerful self-buffs makes them a dominant force in PvE and PvP. Known for their ferocious critical hits and ability to endure high amounts of damage, the Titan is the perfect class for those who want to lead the charge and crush their enemies with brute force.

Grand Khavatari
is a master of martial arts and rapid, relentless attacks. This class specializes in using fist weapons and focuses on quick, successive strikes to overwhelm opponents. With powerful self-buffs and abilities that increase their attack speed, critical rate, and damage, the Grand Khavatari can tear through enemies with lightning-fast combos. They are also highly mobile, making them dangerous adversaries in both PvE and PvP, capable of wearing down foes with sheer speed and precision.

Dominator
is a fearsome orc shaman who leads the battlefield with powerful clan-wide buffs and debuffs. Their unique ability to empower entire clans with offensive and defensive buffs makes them an essential leader in mass battles. Additionally, Dominators can unleash powerful debuffs and damage spells, weakening enemies and controlling the battlefield. Their role in combat is to support their allies while crippling their enemies, excelling in large-scale PvP, where their influence can turn the tide of war.

Doomcryer
is a versatile battle shaman, combining potent offensive magic with powerful buffs and debuffs. They are known for enhancing their party's combat potential and disrupting enemies with debilitating debuffs. Their unique mix of skills allows them to be effective in both solo and group play, providing support while also being able to inflict significant damage. In PvP, they are a force to be reckoned with, capable of buffing allies while simultaneously weakening their opponents.
