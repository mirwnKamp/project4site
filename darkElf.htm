Shillien Templar
is a fierce tank class that harnesses the power of darkness to protect their allies. Combining high defense with offensive dark magic, they can absorb damage, taunt enemies, and unleash powerful debuffs to weaken their foes. Their defensive abilities and crowd control make them an essential frontline protector, excelling in both PvP and PvE environments, where they shield their team while dealing steady damage.
Chain Strike:

Swing a chain to pull the target to you.Requires sword or blunt weapon.

Freezing Strike:

Freezing Strike have been reworked. Freezes the surrounding air inflicting water damage on the enemy, decreasing their speed -20% for 20second. This is a very fast skill.

Shield of Faith:

For 15sec, party members P.Def +3600, M.Def +2700. For the duration absorbs 90% of any damage recieved by a party member.

Shield Deflect Magic:

For 8 sec, Transfers magical damage back to the enemy caster.

Mass Lightning Strike:

Summons a dark lightning that strikes the target and nearby enemies paralyzing them for 5 sec. Requires sword or blunt weapon.

Shield Bash:

Shield Bash have been reworked. Inflicts stun on the target for 4sec, and cancels enemy's target. Requires a shield.

Magical Mirror:

Magical Mirror have been reworked. Reflects physical buffs & debuffs back to the enemy with 30% chance. Requires a shield.

Templar's Aura:

For you and your party members P./M. Attack +10%, P./M. Def +20%, speed +10 & P./M. Skill Cooldown -7%. Only one aura can be active at any time.
