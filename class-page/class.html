Phoenix Knight
The Phoenix Knight excels in absorbing damage and keeping enemies focused on them rather than their more vulnerable teammates. With high defense, powerful self-healing abilities, and skills that reduce the damage taken by themselves and their allies, this class is designed to be a formidable front-line protector.
