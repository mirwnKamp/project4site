Server Ranking

The Server Ranking resets every 15 days, giving players a fresh chance to claim the top spots. The most skilled and active players will earn exclusive buffs that offer a significant advantage in both PvP and PvE. PvP points are awarded to every party member when an enemy is defeated, encouraging teamwork and rewarding support roles. At the end of each 15-day cycle, all PvP points are reset, so staying active and dominating the battlefield is key to securing and maintaining your rank and title.
